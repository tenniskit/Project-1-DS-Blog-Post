  1. Once a decade
  2. Once every few years,
  3. Once a year,
  4. Every few months.

In first three groups, the rate of number of people of job seeking status are the same, even the total numbers of people are different. However, in the last group "Every few months", the number of people who were actively looking for new job is higher. It proves that the people who looking for new job, are willing to learn frequently.

Step 5 Evaluation¶

Finally the three diagrams show us the relations between NEWLearn and JobSeek, Age and Hobbyist.¶

Step 6 Deploy (Communication)¶

After looking at the summary table, we got three findings:

1. From the first diagram "NEWLearn VS JobSeek", we saw that people in the group of "Study new technologies every few months" tend to more willingness to actively look for a job.¶

2. From the second diagram "NEWLearn VS Age", we saw that the people with higher frequency of studying new technologies, they are younger.¶

3. From the third diagram "NEWLearn VS Hobbyist", we found the positive relation between the frequency of studying new technologies and if the person is hobbyist. When people with higher chance of being hobbyist of programming, their frequency of studying new technologies is higher.¶
